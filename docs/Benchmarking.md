| Model                                    | Primary Purpose                                                | Advantages                                                                                                | Limitations                                                     | Selection Rationale                                                                                          |
| ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Variational Autoencoder (VAE)**        | Learn a shared latent representation of review embeddings      | Learns compact semantic representations, enables smooth latent space interpolation                        | May generate less detailed or blurry representations            | Selected to encode review headlines and bodies into a shared latent space for downstream generation.         |
| **Transformer (T5 + LoRA)**              | Generate review headlines and review bodies                    | Produces fluent, context-aware text, benefits from pretrained weights, LoRA enables efficient fine-tuning | Requires significant computational resources for long sequences | Selected for high-quality conditional text generation while reducing training cost through LoRA fine-tuning. |
| **Diffusion Model**                      | Refine latent representations and improve text quality         | Produces semantically consistent and diverse outputs, improves generation quality                         | Slower inference due to iterative denoising                     | Selected to refine latent embeddings generated by the VAE and improve review coherence.                      |
| **Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)** | Assess and improve the realism of generated reviews            | Encourages realistic and diverse outputs through adversarial learning                                     | Training can be unstable and sensitive to hyperparameters       | Selected to evaluate and enhance the realism of generated reviews using a discriminator.                     |
| **Normalizing Flow**                     | Predict review helpfulness and model conditional distributions | Exact likelihood estimation, flexible probability modeling, invertible transformations                    | Computationally expensive for complex architectures             | Selected to model review helpfulness scores and improve controllability of the generated reviews.            |
